MGU ACM

ACM brings together computing educators, researchers, and professionals to inspire dialogue, share resources, and address the field's challenges. Mahatma Gandhi University ACM Students' Chapter is an official ACM students chapter founded on January 4, 2021. We are a team of enthusiastic, creative, curious, ambitious people seeking the experience of the professional environment.

Mission

The chapter aims at serving as a vibrant hub of activities for the computing network at Mahatma Gandhi University, Kottayam, where enthusiasts meet, interact and learn from each other.

Vision

The chapter focuses to build a network of coders and professionals, to offer resources to young aspiring minds to shape their future. The chapter plans various events which include lecture series, technical workshops, technical talk, conferences etc.
